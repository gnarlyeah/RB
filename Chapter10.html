Chapter 10: The Statue

I am but a humble servant,
Ever waiting by your side.
Though our love may yet be hidden,
I am willing to abide.
    
For I know that in your heart,
There lies a love so true.
But society's harsh strictures,
Have kept it locked from view.
    
But fear not, my dearest love,
For I will wait forever.
I'll keep our flame burning bright,
In secret and in fever.
    
For when the time is right,
And you are ready to be free,
I'll be here, my love,
For you and only thee.
    
So let us bide our time,
Until the world is kinder,
For I will love you always,
My dear and faithful sinder.
