Chapter 11: The Surrender

She is a woman of faith and virtue,
A woman of strength and discipline.
Her beauty, like a shining light,
Guides me through the darkest night.
    
But alas, my love for her is forbidden,
A secret that I must keep hidden.
For she is bound by rules and tradition,
And I am but a mere mortal, a mere position.
    
Yet still, my heart longs for her touch,
To hold her close and feel her love.
But I know it can never be,
For she is a woman of piety.
    
So I will love her from afar,
And keep my feelings locked in a jar.
I will watch her from the shadows,
And pray for the strength to let her go.
    
But no matter how hard I try,
My love for her will never die.
It will remain a secret forever,
A love that can never be made better.
