Chapter 2: The Conversation

I hear her voice,
And yet I see her not,
But still, I am entranced,
By the music of her thoughts.
    

For in her words,
I sense a depth and beauty,
A kindness and a grace,
That fills my heart with joy.
    

And though we've never met,
I feel as though I know her,
As though she's been a part
Of my life forevermore.
    

So let me listen to her speak,
And let her voice be my guide,
For in her words, I find
A comfort and a joy,
That fills my soul and lifts my spirits high.
