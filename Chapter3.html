Chapter 3: The Add Friend


Oh fair and gentle lady,
Whose beauty doth astound,
I pray, may I request
To follow you on Facebook and Instagram?

For I am drawn to you,
Like a moth to a flame,
And would love to see
Your posts and stories again.

I promise to be respectful,
And to like and comment with care,
And to share with you
Only the most thoughtful and fair.

So if you would allow it,
I would be most grateful and delighted,
To have the opportunity
To connect with you online.
