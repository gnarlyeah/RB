Chapter 4: The Angelic Voice from Wesing

Her voice is like a sweet, clear bell,
Ringing out across the land,
A sound that fills my heart with joy,
And sets my soul alight.

As she sings on WeSing,
I am mesmerized by her talent,
Her pitch and tone so pure and true,
Her words full of emotion and feeling.
    
Her voice is like a balm,
Healing all my wounds and cares,
And lifting my spirits high,
As I listen to her melodies.
    
So let me sing along with her,
And let our voices rise as one,
In a duet of joy and harmony,
As we make beautiful music together.
