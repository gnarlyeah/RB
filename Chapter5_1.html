Chapter 5.1: The Shock

Oh, how my heart was struck with fear,
To learn that she, the one I hold dear,
Is a nun, devoted to a life of faith,
And to the service of the Lord.
    
For I had thought her free to love,
And open to all the joys thereof,
But now I see that she is bound,
By vows and duties that must come before.
    
Yet even as I struggle with this news,
I cannot help but admire her,
For she has chosen a path of selflessness,
And dedicated herself to a higher cause.
    
So let me honor her,
And let her be an inspiration to me,
To live a life of purpose and meaning,
And to follow my own true path.
