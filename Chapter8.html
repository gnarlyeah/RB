Chapter 8: Radiance

I saw her today, my heart skipped a beat
She was even more beautiful, more holy and sweet
In person, her presence commanded respect
And I couldn't help but feel my love, unchecked
    
A glimmer of warmth, a hint of a smile
It was then that I knew, I'd go the extra mile
To be near her, to bask in her glow
For I was hopelessly smitten, head over heels, don't you know
    
She may be religious, conservative and strict
But to me, she is perfect, and I can't resist
The pull towards her, the desire to be
By her side, forever, my love, oh blessed nun, you'll always be.
