Chapter 1: The Voice

Oh, sweetest sound that ever met my ear,
A voice so pure and clear,
Like a bell ringing out across the mead,
Bringing joy and hope, a new lease on life.
    

For when I hear her speak,
My heart doth leap,
And all my doubts and fears
Are swept away by the music of her words.
    

Her voice is like a balm,
Healing all the wounds that life has caused,
And filling me with a sense of peace and joy
That I have never known before.
    

So let me bask in the warmth of her voice,
And let it guide me through this world of woe,
For in her words I find the strength to carry on,
And the hope of a brighter tomorrow.
